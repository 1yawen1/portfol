Parkin. NCKU

2022 | iOS & Android App | UI/UX Design

Introduction

On a busy school day, there are often no seats in the underground parking lot at National Cheng Kung University.

Therefore, we designed the parking reservation system parkin.ncku to make users more time-saving and convenient.

What I did

  • Interface Design
  • Figma Wireframe
  • Process development


Discover

Empathy Map

Based on our own experience, we discussed in the group the problems and feelings we encountered when parking.


How might we?

Brainstorming

We proposed solutions for the above four scenarios, and finally decided to develop the design with a "parking reservation system".
