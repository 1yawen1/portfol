Introduction

A singing app specially designed for Taiwanese,

allowing you to practice singing easily even if you don’t speak Taiwanese.

What I did

  • Interface Design
  • Figma Wireframe
  • Process development

problem

story board

User story

For users who want to learn Taiwanese songs but are not familiar with Taiwanese, we provide a singing app with Taiwanese teaching. Learning Taiwanese can also achieve entertainment effects.


Difine

Our Goal



. Promote Taiwanese language learning.
. Provide a platform to practice singing.

Prototype

#1 Search Taiwanese songs

Search popular Taiwanese songs so you can practice singing easily at home.


#2 Singing mode

Dynamic lyrics guide, sentence-by-sentence translation and instant viewing of Chinese and Taiwanese comparisons.


#3 Record

Through recording, you can practice repeatedly and share it with friends for guidance.
